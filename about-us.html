Who We Are

Barrydunnroofing.co.uk is an independent casino review and comparison website based in Weston-super-Mare, Somerset. Our mission is straightforward: to help UK players make informed decisions about where to play online by providing honest, thoroughly researched reviews that prioritise transparency over hype.

We started this project because we were frustrated with the state of casino review websites. Too many sites publish reviews that read like adverts, recommending every casino equally and glossing over genuine shortcomings. We take a different approach. Every casino on our site is tested with real accounts, real deposits, and real withdrawals. If a site falls short, we say so.

Our team brings together experience from across the UK gambling industry — from compliance and regulation to player advocacy and game analysis. We are not affiliated with any single casino operator, which means our recommendations are based on genuine merit rather than commercial relationships.

Our Review Process

Every casino review begins with the same process. We create an account, verify our identity, make a deposit, claim any available bonus, and play through real wagering requirements. We then request a withdrawal and time how long it takes to receive our funds. We contact customer support with genuine questions and assess how quickly and helpfully they respond. We test the mobile experience on both iOS and Android devices. And we review the responsible gambling tools to ensure they are accessible and functional.

This process takes time — typically several weeks per casino — but it means every statement we publish is something we have personally verified. We do not rely on second-hand information, manufacturer claims, or press releases.

Editorial Independence

Our website uses affiliate links, which means we may earn a commission when readers sign up to a casino through our links. This is how we fund our operations and keep the site free to use. However, our editorial process is completely independent of our commercial relationships. A casino cannot pay for a higher ranking or a more favourable review. Our recommendations are based solely on our testing results.
